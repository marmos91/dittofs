<objective>
Relocate the `ComputeObjectID` invocation out of Syncer's `persistFileBlocksAfterFlush` and into `rollup.go`'s post-`SetRollupOffset` hook. After this plan, local-only shares (no remote configured) produce non-zero ObjectIDs at rollup time — closing the Phase 13 UAT surprise where ObjectIDs only materialized after remote upload.

Purpose: ObjectID is a property of the BlockRef manifest, not of remote upload — decoupling the compute from Syncer is the structural correction that makes the rest of Phase 18 (delete persistFileBlocksAfterFlush in Plan 06) safe.

Output: New FSStoreOptions slot for the persister callback, rollup.go modified to accumulate BlockRefs in the chunker loop and invoke ComputeObjectID + persister after SetRollupOffset, new test covering happy path and nil-persister fallthrough.
</objective>
